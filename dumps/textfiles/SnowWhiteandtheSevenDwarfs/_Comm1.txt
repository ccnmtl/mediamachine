Snow White was the first animated feature film created by Disney in 1937 and while technically, it represented the most advanced work of its kind at the time, thematically; it represents a female cipher that has existed through the ages. Snow White is sweet, kind and cute-in short, inoffensive. She never really does anything, save care for the dwarfs as if they were small children. However, even in this role as a caretaker, we know as viewers that she is just killing time while waiting for her prince.<br>-JMB
