Torn Curtain is one of Alfred Hitchcock's least effective films, despite the presence of stars Paul Newman and Julie Andrews. Newman plays Michael Armstrong, an American scientist who pretends to be a defector to East Germany so he can discover the details of the Soviet's nuclear missiles. Following Armstrong to East Germany is his secretary and fiancee Sarah Sherman (Andrews). After discovering the missile secrets, the pair have to overcome a number of dangerous obstacles before they can escape Germany. - Stephen Thomas Erlewine<br>(Allmovie.com)
