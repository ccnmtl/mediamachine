Sebastian Caine (Kevin Bacon) and his team of scientists are about to make history as he becomes the first human to become invisible. As he transforms, his body endures not only the bizarre phenomenon of becoming totally transparent, then fading into nothing, but also tremendous physical pain. Once he disappears, quiet calm washes over and the smug scientists rest on their laurels for a moment. But who has more power now, those with the ability to make others visible and invisible or those who cannot be seen? (jlh)
