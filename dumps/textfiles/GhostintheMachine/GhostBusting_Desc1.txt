As Terry Munroe (Karen Allen) puts her son Josh (Wil Horneff) to sleep, she has no idea that a serial killer who has been absorbed into her town's computer network is attempting to invade her home. In the form of waves of energy and sound, the killer stalks Terry and her son and terrorizes their poor dog as well.<br><br>Although the haunting of one's answering machine seems a bit odd, this type of occupation by an electronic medium seems somehow more tangible than a traditional haunting by a spirit or ghost. That a serial killer can be condensed into electronic form and pass through telephone wires, fuse boxes and other everyday means places us somewhat at the mercy of our own technologies. (jlh)<br>
