After a terrible air disaster, survivor Max Klein (Jeff Bridges) emerges a changed person. Unable to connect to his former life or to wife Laura (Isabella Rosellini), he feels godlike and invulnerable. When psychologist Bill Perlman (John Turturro) is unable to help Max, he has Max meet another survivor, Carla Rodrigo (Rosie Perez), who is prostate from grief and guilt since her baby died in the crash which she and Max survived. <br><br>Based on the novel by Rafael Yglesias<br><br>
