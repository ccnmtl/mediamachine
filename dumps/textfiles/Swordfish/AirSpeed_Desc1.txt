Shot in flesh-tingling close-up, this tense, rather mean spirited movie reveals fascinating parallels to our era of volatile urban terrorist panic, although it was completed before 9-11.  A parolee hacker (Hugh Jackman) takes on a challenging assignment masterminded by a ruthless hard guy (John Travolta) to steal electronically a government slush fund of 10 million -- codename &#34;Swordfish&#34; (cf. the Marx Bros.)   Halle Berry (woefully exploited) is the undercover double agent.<br><br>In this scene,  the hostages, surviving the first debacle, are packed into a high speed bus, which is then airlifted to avoid the police. The scenes that ensue are strange prescident with regard to traumatic imagery later relayed from the events of 9-11.<br><br>-JMB
