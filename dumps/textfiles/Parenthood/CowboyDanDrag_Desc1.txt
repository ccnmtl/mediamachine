In this clip, Steve Martin enacts almost the entire curriculum of Lacan's theory of child development from the point of view of the encounter between boy and man, boy and girl, boy and mother, boy and self -- in short, the self confronting "the other".  The birthday party is a very serious, life and death ritual for the growing son who wishes to be initiated into manhood by someone other than his father, but in crushing reality finds it is his father in drag who does the trick.<br><br>
