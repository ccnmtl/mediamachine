A vertiginous selection of clips from Rock Hudson's movies, focusing not on his stellar Hollywood career, but his doubles entendres, quips, and give aways that supposedly allowed him to let on surreptitiously that he was gay, without coming out of the closet and ruining his career.  Narrated by the director. An "impudetly insightful ... subversive delight," according to Duane Byrge of `The Hollywood Reporter.'<br>-JMB<br><br>
