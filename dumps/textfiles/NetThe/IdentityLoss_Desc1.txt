On a whim, computer hacker Angela Bennett (Sandra Bullock) decides to escape the thrall of life on the screen for a Caribbean vacation. Unfortunately, her specialist knowledge plunges her into a dangerous affair and near death experience at sea. The big-time electronic crooks pursuing her succeed in erasing her identity, her credit cards, her car, and her home. <br><br>In this clip, a bedraggled Angela returns from her thrilling but life-threatening holiday, still in shock, goes to the airport to get her flight home. An alternative identity is supplied for her by an airline official: the itinerant persona of the fictitious but aptly named &#34;Ruth Marx.&#34; With or without the help of her ranting ex-shrink (the egregiously sweaty Dennis Miller), she attempts to turn the tables on her assailants.
