In this clip, Dawn is giving a speech on the merits of dignity and the ignobility of "grade grubbing." Ironically, Dawn is humiliating herself by lecturing her peers on this taboo subject, which she realizes is reducing in the eyes of her peers any shred of dignity she might have once held.
