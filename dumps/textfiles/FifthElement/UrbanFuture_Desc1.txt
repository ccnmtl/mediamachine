The Fifth Element (Milla Jovovich), a woman named Leeloo, has just arrived on Earth and is terrified by the chaos around her. Immediately seized by the police, who are bewildered that she has no discernable identity according to their technology, she jumps from a building to escape persecution.<br><br>The Fifth Element, or perfect being, is to be united with four stones representing earth, air, fire and water in order to prevent the onslaught of evil from an alien force. However, here we see that this perfect being seems vulnerable and afraid, alienated by the technology that surrounds her and desperate to escape her fate rather than embrace the quest she is meant to take.
